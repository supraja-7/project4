Big Data

Big data refers to extremely large and diverse collections of structured,
unstructured, and semi-structured data that continues to grow exponentially over time.
Big data is the set of technologies created to store, analyse and manage this bulk data,
a macro-tool created to identify patterns in the chaos of this explosion in information in
order to design smart solutions. Today it is used in areas as diverse as medicine, agriculture, gambling and environmental protection.
